INTRODUCTION

Like Campus of IIT KGP, The total number of Societies and clubs is also prominent in number. There are 50+ Clubs and societies in total, covering most of the fields. This gives a variety of options to students. There is no restriction that one student can only opt for one or any limited number of clubs/societies. You can opt for as many as you can hold it. Clubs and Socities give you a broader option to Explore, So don't miss it, Check out every society and club and join accordingly. Never miss the chance.
